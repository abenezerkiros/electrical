Frequently Asked Questions

Why is professional ceiling fan installation important?

Professional installation ensures your ceiling fan operates safely and efficiently. Our certified electricians handle everything from secure mounting to proper electrical connections, preventing potential hazards and ensuring optimal performance for years to come.

How long does a typical ceiling fan installation take?

Most standard ceiling fan installations are completed within 2-3 hours. However, this timeframe may vary depending on your ceiling type, existing wiring, and whether additional supports are needed for secure mounting.

Do you provide the ceiling fan or should I purchase one?

We offer both options! You can choose from our selection of high-quality, energy-efficient ceiling fans, or we can install a fan you've already purchased. Our team will ensure it's compatible with your space and electrical system.

Can you install ceiling fans in rooms without existing fixtures?

Yes, we can install ceiling fans in rooms without existing fixtures. This process requires running new wiring and potentially cutting a new opening in the ceiling, which our experienced electricians handle with minimal disruption to your home.

Do you offer a warranty on your installation work?

Absolutely! All our ceiling fan installations come with a 2-year workmanship guarantee. We stand behind the quality of our work and ensure every installation meets the highest safety standards and regulations.
